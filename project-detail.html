The Challenge

Old system,
new expectations.

Nebula's existing app had a 2.1-star rating on the App Store. Users complained about confusing navigation, buried key actions, and a visual design that felt dated and untrustworthy. The product team needed a complete overhaul — not a facelift.

The Approach

Research first,
design always.

We ran 18 user interviews, analysed heatmaps from 6 months of session recordings, and audited 12 competitor apps. Every design decision in the final product traces back to a specific user insight or business goal.
