FAQ

Common questions.

What's your typical project process?

Discovery call → Proposal & SOW → Design sprints → Feedback loops → Final delivery. I keep you in the loop at every stage.

Do you work with clients remotely?

Absolutely. 100% of my client work is remote. I use Figma, Loom, and Notion to keep collaboration seamless regardless of timezone.

How long do projects usually take?

Brand identities: 3–5 weeks. UX/UI projects: 6–14 weeks. Social media systems: 2–4 weeks. Timelines depend on scope and revisions.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes — every project includes structured revision rounds built into the timeline. I'd rather get it perfect than rush to delivery.
